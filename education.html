2020-2023 (february)

UiTM Machang

  • After SPM, I continued my studies at the Diploma level.
  • I got a Diploma in Information Management at UiTM Kelantan, Machang campus based on the results of UPU.
  • It takes 2½ years (5 semesters) to complete the diploma.
  • From semester 1 to semester 4, online distance learning (ODL) was conducted due to the COVID-19 epidemic that hit the country.
  • Semester 5 learning is done in a hybrid way, which is ODL and face-to-face. During the 5th semester we were able to meet our classmates and lecturers.
  • Although only one semester was done hybridly, I am grateful for the many experiences and lessons I got in that one semester.

    • 2015-2019

    SMK Puncak Alam

  • I continued my secondary school education at SMK Puncak Alam.
  • The distance between my house and the school is 5km and I take the bus to school.
  • I took PT3 (Pentaksiran Tingkatan 3) and SPM (Sijil Pelajaran Malaysia) at this school.
  • I have held the position of librarian at this school since I was in the form 3.
  • I have many friends in this school who are good and help each other.
  • There are many memories that I cannot forget in this school, and it is the best school for me.

    • 2012-2014

    SK Puncak Alam 2

  • My family and I moved to Puncak Alam when I was 10 years old.
  • I continue primary school at SK Puncak Alam 2. It is quite far from my house and I go to school by bus.
  • Since I attended school here, my academic and extracurricular performance has been improving. It is because of the encouragement of parents, teachers, and friends who support each other.
  • I took the UPSR (Ujian Penilaian Sekolah Rendah) at this school and got satisfactory results for me.

    • 2009-2011

    SK Taman Putra Perdana 2

  • When I was 7 to 9 years old, I went to primary school at SK Taman Putra Perdana 2.
  • It is located at Puchong, Selangor and I have many friends in this school.

    • 2008

    PASTI

  • When I was 6 years old, I started my education at Pusat Asuhan Tunas Islam (PASTI), which is an Islamic pre-school.
  • It is located near my house which is in Taman Putra Perdana, Puchong, Selangor.
  • It was at Pusat Asuhan Tunas Islam (PASTI) that I started making friends and my childhood began here.
